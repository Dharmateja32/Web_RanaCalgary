    Welcome to RANA Calgary

    A RESPONSIBILITY TOWARDS THE PAST AND THE FUTURE

    With these pearls of wisdom in perspective, a small group of visionary Rajasthanis, put their heart and soul together to provide a forum for all Rajasthanis and other Indians living in Calgary to effectively nurture and cultivate love and respect amongst each other by forming this organization.

    Non-Profit Organization

    RANA Calgary is a non-profit organization, energized by members providing a dedicated platform to create a stronger sense of community among the Indo-Canadian of Rajasthan descent and the people from other part of India.

    RANA Calgary is driving forces to promote propagates and preserves the incredibly rich Rajasthan culture, heritage, traditions and values, and simultaneously instill these in the present and future generations.

    Establishment

    RANA Calgary was founded in 2012 by a visionary founder and Past President ‘Yogesh Paliwal’ along with other dedicated team of Patrons, Directors, Conveners and Members and progressing towards attending its objective.

    We encourage everyone to become a part of this fantastics organization …Together we’ll grow our traditions and culture and prosper on a path shown by lord Krishna.
